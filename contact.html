    What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

      IT professionals can be involved in many activities such as responding to technical queries raised by customers, updating new software application, website release for customers, hack the matrix, and helping with server issues

    What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with?

      There a wide range of people that IT professionals may interact with: tooling and automation developer, infrastructure analyst, cloud security engineer, system admin, and their coworker. However, they rarely interact with clients as clients usually don't understand what can that technology does.

    Where do the IT professionals spend most of their time?

      They spend most of their time working in the office. However, some can choose to work at home

    What aspect of their position is most challenging?

      The information technology industry has many career paths, so it is normal to change their careers. Although this is trouble for IT specialist as it takes time to get used to their new job
