    Team profile

      As the personality test result, finding a team member base on the first impression in spirit and attitude is what I tend to do, as these are vital elements that affect internal relationships in a team. And though the assignment development process, team member needs to build and place trust in each other, this would help substantially in the period before the deadline as team members usually get stress out during this period. Secondly, the ideal team member that I need is also required to have a different opinion from other team members to contribute to the project, good communication skills, and responsible to hand in the work on time this can help by establishing communication connection. Moreover, a dream team in my opinion is not a team that has a strong point in one aspect but rather has a well-balanced in all attribute divided throughout all team member. The most crucial factor when forming a team is to have a team leader who has a wide range of knowledge in many aspects that can be a mentor and an emotional supporter. As stated above, these are the requirement of my dream team and this arrangement would work best for me when developing a project.
