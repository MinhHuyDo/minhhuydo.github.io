    Project Ideas

    Overview

      The project idea is to create a healthy tracker app that allows users to easily create meals for the family base on nutrition value and preferences. This application will be build base on AI and cloud computing technology to ensure the quality of application support and reduce the cost of building the application. With this project, users can save time, effort in planning meals for their families. They can easily plan and calculate the number of ingredients that need to buy with a single touch.

    Description

      The goal of this project is to develop a mobile app that will enable you to conveniently select nutritious dishes for your family. Using the application can help you calculate nutritional ingredients in food by weight in just seconds. By choosing family size, gender, age, and country, the app will show you the proportion of ingredients that you need to buy to balance the nutrition and ensure the goal that you have select for your family. By using this application, user can improve the variety of dishes and your cooking skill with the help of AI technology, dishes that you have previously made will be store in your preferences list for it to recommend based on your previous selections. Besides choosing recipes from the AI recommendation system, you can also select recipes that are shared online in the community. Additional features will be added for future progress,e.g, weight tracker, home workout plan, and water reminder.
